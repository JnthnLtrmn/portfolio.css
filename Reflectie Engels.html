Reflection

Strong points

  • Analyzing: One of my strengths is analyzing code and studying step by step what when happens. This way I also look at how it is laid out and can determine how something is put together.
  • Eager to learn: Learning about something in an ever-changing world is a big plus that I carry with me. To learn something is a motivation that stimulates me. Also to update existing knowledge gives me a motivation.
  • Structure: For me, structure is a way of working logically. By working in a structured way you keep an overview of what you are doing. If there is a problem, it can also be easier to search for a solution of the problem.

Work points

  • Self-effective: I misjudge myself where the result usually comes out better than what I thought myself had. the result can also be wrong, but I learn from this by looking at what I have to do next time pay attention.
  • Rudderless: Because I prefer to work step by step, it becomes difficult for me to determine how to start a project. My knowledge of programming is still limited for this, but this will improve by working more with this.
  • Concretize: Due to my limited knowledge, I do not always know what should be used where and when. Because of this I can sometimes get stuck, which means that time is lost looking for work.
